Our mission
what is My Struggle

My Struggle is a nonprofit founded in 2021 in Laveen, Arizona, by people who have lived through homelessness, addiction, and incarceration — and found their way home.

We believe nobody escapes the struggle alone. Our outreach centers pair every member with a peer mentor who has walked the same road, because the person best equipped to guide you out is someone who has been where you are.

Through QR Code Giving, anyone can give directly to a member's journey — half in cash they redeem at our centers, half in credits for essentials at The Store. Every gift is accountable, dignified, and personal.

Our register is simple: "How can I help you? Let's talk — tell me about you." Compassion replacing contempt, one member at a time.

FAQ
Questions, answered
Where does my donation actually go? +−
When you give to a member, your gift splits 50/50: half becomes cash they redeem in person at our outreach centers with their member ID card, and half becomes Store Credits for essentials at The Store. Members can save either balance for their reentry into society.
How does QR Code Giving work? +−
Every member carries a personal QR code. Scan it with your phone camera and you land on their giving page — their story, their goals, and a secure checkout. Your gift reaches them in under a minute, split 50/50 between center cash and Store Credits. See Danielle's page →
Is my donation tax-deductible? +−
My Struggle is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. You'll receive a receipt by email for every gift; deductibility details for member-directed gifts are included on the receipt.
How do I become a mentor? +−
Apply in five minutes — a center coordinator calls you within a week. Lived experience is the qualification; we provide training, the mentor app, and staff support. Start your application →
